Tourist Places in Bihar

Bodh Gaya

A major city known for the Brahmaputra River, Kamakhya Temple, and vibrant culture.

Patna

Famous for its traditional silk weaving industry and scenic beauty along the Brahmaputra.

Nalanda

Known for its mythology, historical temples, and the scenic beauty along the Brahmaputra.

Vaishali

Famous for its tea gardens, Assam's tea capital, and historical importance in Ahom history.

Rajgir

The world's largest river island, known for its vibrant culture, Vaishnavite monasteries, and natural beauty.

Madhubani

A historic town with Ahom dynasty monuments like Rang Ghar and Talatal Ghar.

Sitamarhi

Known for tea plantations and as a gateway to Arunachal Pradesh's scenic destinations.

Bhagalpur

Offers breathtaking landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and rich tribal cultures.

Pawapuri

Famous for being the home of the endangered one-horned rhinoceros and diverse wildlife.

Muzaffarpur

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its biodiversity and scenic beauty.

Hajipur

Assam's only hill station, surrounded by rolling hills, lakes, and rich cultural heritage.
