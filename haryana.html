Tourist Places in Haryana

Gurgaon

Gurgaon, now known as Gurugram, is famous for its skyscrapers, bustling malls, and as a business hub of Haryana.

Kurukshetra

Kurukshetra is a historic city known for its significance in the Mahabharata war and the holy Brahma Sarovar.

Nuh

Nuh is known for its heritage sites like the Tomb of Sheikh Musa and has a rich Mughal history.

Karnal

Karnal, often referred to as the 'Rice Bowl of India', is known for its history and agricultural research centers.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is a popular destination for bird watchers, home to numerous migratory birds.

Morni Hills

Morni Hills is a serene hill station in Haryana, ideal for trekking, adventure sports, and a peaceful getaway.

Damdama Lake

Damdama Lake is a popular picnic spot known for boating, adventure activities, and its scenic surroundings.

Manesar

Manesar is an industrial town known for its rapidly growing infrastructure and cultural tourism.

Murthal

Murthal is famous for its roadside dhabas offering delicious parathas, making it a popular food destination.

Kalesar National Park

Kalesar National Park is a wildlife haven with dense forests, perfect for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Chandigarh

Chandigarh, the shared capital of Haryana and Punjab, is renowned for its urban planning and modern architecture.

Panipat

Panipat is known for its rich history, including the historic battles fought here, and its thriving handloom industry.
