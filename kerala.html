Tourist Places in Kerala

Alleppey

Known for its serene backwaters and houseboat cruises, Alleppey is a top destination for relaxation and scenic beauty.

Wayanad

A green paradise, Wayanad is famous for its spice plantations, wildlife, and picturesque landscapes.

Cochin

A historical port city, Cochin blends traditional culture with modernity and offers famous landmarks like Fort Kochi.

Munnar

Munnar is a hill station renowned for its tea gardens, misty mountains, and cool climate.

Kumarakom

Another beautiful backwater destination, Kumarakom offers bird watching and houseboat stays amidst nature.

Kovalam

Famous for its crescent-shaped beaches, Kovalam is ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and experiencing coastal life.

Thekkady

Thekkady is home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where visitors can enjoy safaris and see elephants and other wildlife.

Vagamon

A lesser-known hill station, Vagamon offers tranquility and is ideal for trekking and paragliding adventures.

Kozhikode

Historically significant for spice trade, Kozhikode boasts cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, and vibrant markets.

Ashtamudi

A picturesque lake known for its houseboat cruises and proximity to traditional villages.

Bekal

Bekal is known for its majestic fort and scenic beach, offering a glimpse into Kerala's rich history.

Guruvayur

A major pilgrimage site, Guruvayur is famous for its ancient Krishna temple and spiritual ambiance.
