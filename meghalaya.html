Tourist Places in Meghalaya

Cherrapunji

Known for its rainfall, Cherrapunji is a beautiful place with breathtaking landscapes, living root bridges, and lush greenery.

Balpakram National Park

This scenic national park is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, known as the 'Land of Spirits' by locals.

Shillong

Nicknamed the 'Scotland of the East,' Shillong is known for its charming hills, lakes, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Living Root Bridge

These unique bridges made from tree roots are iconic to Meghalaya and offer an extraordinary sight and adventure for visitors.

Umiam Lake

A popular spot for water sports and picnics, Umiam Lake is surrounded by scenic hills and serene views.

Tura

Tura offers stunning landscapes and rich biodiversity, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

Nohkalikai Falls

One of the tallest waterfalls in India, Nohkalikai Falls is an awe-inspiring sight surrounded by beautiful landscapes.

Mawlynnong Village

Often called the cleanest village in Asia, Mawlynnong is known for its well-maintained environment and warm hospitality.

Krem Liat Prah

The longest cave system in India, Krem Liat Prah is a must-visit for caving enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
