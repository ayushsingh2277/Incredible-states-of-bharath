Tourist Places in Nagaland

Dimapur

Known for its historical ruins and vibrant markets, Dimapur is the gateway to Nagaland.

Kohima

The capital city, famous for the World War II Cemetery and beautiful landscapes.

Mokokchung

Known as the cultural center of the Ao tribe, Mokokchung is celebrated for its festivals and heritage.

Mon

Home to the fascinating Konyak tribe, known for their warrior culture and traditional tattoos.

Wokha

Famous for its scenic beauty, hilltop landscapes, and the colorful Hornbill Festival.

Dzukou Valley

Renowned for its stunning seasonal flowers, this valley is ideal for trekking and nature exploration.

Tuophema Village

A traditional village offering a cultural immersion experience with local food and homestays.

Kiphire

A destination for adventure lovers, featuring Mount Saramati, the highest peak in Nagaland.

Tuensang

Known for its ethnic diversity, Tuensang offers insights into the culture of several tribes.
