Delicious Punjabi Cuisine

Punjabi cuisine is known for its rich, buttery flavors, generous use of dairy products, and hearty dishes. It's a reflection of the region's agricultural abundance and the robust lifestyle of its people. Let's explore some iconic Punjabi dishes!

Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy curry made with tender chicken pieces in a tomato-based sauce.

Sarson da Saag

A vegetarian dish made from mustard greens, often served with makki di roti (cornbread).

Cholle Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried bread, a popular breakfast and street food.

Amritsari Fish

Crispy fried fish, usually made with Singara fish, coated in a spicy batter.

Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a tandoor (clay oven).

Makki di Roti & Sarson da Saag

Cornbread served with a curry made from mustard greens, a classic Punjabi combination.
