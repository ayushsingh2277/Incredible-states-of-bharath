Famous Foods of Punjab

Explore the rich and flavorful cuisine of Punjab!

Amritsari Kulcha

A popular Punjabi dish, Amritsari Kulcha is a stuffed flatbread filled with spiced potatoes and herbs, cooked to perfection in a tandoor.

Sarson ka Saag & Makki di Roti

This iconic combination features mustard greens cooked with spices, served with flatbread made from cornmeal, symbolizing the warmth of Punjab's flavors.

Butter Chicken

A world-famous dish originating from Punjab, Butter Chicken is tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce, rich in flavor and spices.

Lassi

A refreshing drink made from yogurt, Lassi is a popular beverage in Punjab, available in both sweet and salty flavors.
