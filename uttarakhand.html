Tourist Places in Uttarakhand

Jim Corbett National Park

Famous for its Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife, it's a prime destination for wildlife enthusiasts.

Haridwar

One of the holiest cities in India, Haridwar is renowned for its Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.

Rishikesh

Known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World', Rishikesh is famous for its serene ashrams and adventure sports.

Nainital

Known for its beautiful lake, Nainital is a popular hill station with scenic landscapes and boating activities.

Mussoorie

The 'Queen of Hills', Mussoorie offers breathtaking views and is a favorite for honeymooners and families.

Almora

A culturally rich town with stunning views of the Himalayas, Almora is known for its unique handicrafts.

Valley of Flowers

This UNESCO World Heritage site is a paradise with blooming flowers and is ideal for trekking.

Auli

A favorite winter destination, Auli is known for skiing and beautiful mountain vistas.

Munsiyari

Known as 'Little Kashmir', Munsiyari offers a panoramic view of the Panchachuli peaks.
