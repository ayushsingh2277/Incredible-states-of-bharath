Tourist Places in Chhattisgarh

Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Explore the rich biodiversity of Chhattisgarh's renowned wildlife sanctuary, home to exotic species and lush forests.

Raipur

The capital city of Chhattisgarh, known for its cultural heritage and vibrant markets.

Jagdalpur

Famous for the majestic Chitrakote Falls, Jagdalpur offers a blend of natural beauty and tribal culture.

Sirpur

An ancient town with significant archaeological sites, including Buddhist monasteries and temples.

Mainpat

A beautiful hill station known as the 'Mini Tibet' of Chhattisgarh, famous for its scenic views and Tibetan settlements.

Madku Dweep

An island surrounded by rivers, known for its natural beauty and ancient temples.

Dhamtari

A gateway to the Sitanadi and Udanti wildlife sanctuaries, offering adventure and eco-tourism opportunities.

Malhar

Explore ancient temples and relics at this historical site, known for its archaeological significance.

Rajim

A sacred town known for the Rajim Kumbh Mela and its rich cultural herit

Bhoramdeo Temple

Often referred to as the 'Khajuraho of Chhattisgarh', Bhoramdeo Temple is known for its intricate carvings.

Dantewada

Home to the famous Danteshwari Temple and surrounded by natural beauty, Dantewada offers spiritual and cultural insights.

Chirmiri

A quiet hill station, often called the 'Heaven of Chhattisgarh,' surrounded by lush greenery and pleasant climate.
