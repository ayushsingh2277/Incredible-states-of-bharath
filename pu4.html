Pathankot

One of the most famous places in Punjab, Pathankot borders Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir. Blessed with lush greenery, this city looks even more stunning during the monsoon season. Alongside its lovely landscape, the city is known for its history as well. Pathankot also serves as a major railhead to various neighbouring cities in Himachal such as Dalhousie.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Nurpur Fort
  • Mukteshwar Temple
  • Kathgarh Temple
  • Shahpurkandi Fort
  • Ranjit Sagar Dam
