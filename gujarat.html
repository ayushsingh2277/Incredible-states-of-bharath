Tourist Places in Gujarat

Somnath

Somnath is a holy town famous for its Somnath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.

Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and remarkable architecture including the Sabarmati Ashram.

Dwarka

Dwarka is an ancient city known for its Dwarkadhish Temple, one of the four sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

Rann Of Kutch

The Rann of Kutch is a vast white salt desert that comes alive during the Rann Utsav festival with cultural events.

Gir National Park

Gir National Park is the only place in the world where you can spot the majestic Asiatic lions in the wild.

Vadodara

Vadodara is famous for its grand palaces, including the stunning Laxmi Vilas Palace and rich cultural heritage.

Junagadh

Junagadh is a city rich in history, home to Uparkot Fort and the ancient Ashoka Rock Edicts.

Saputara

Saputara is a charming hill station nestled in the Western Ghats, known for its scenic beauty and trekking trails.

Bhuj

Bhuj is a historical city known for its ancient palaces, temples, and being the gateway to the Rann of Kutch.

Rajkot

Rajkot is a vibrant city with a rich history, known for its role in India's independence movement and Gandhian heritage.

Kevadiya

Kevadiya is home to the Statue of Unity, the world's tallest statue, dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.

Modhera Sun Temple

The Modhera Sun Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to the Sun God, known for its stunning architecture.
