Tourist Places in Tripura

Tripura State Museum

Located in Agartala, the Tripura State Museum houses a vast collection of art, culture, and artifacts representing the rich heritage of Tripura.

Agartala

The capital city of Tripura, Agartala is known for its temples, Ujjayanta Palace, and vibrant local markets reflecting the state's culture.

Jagannath Bari

This historic Hindu temple in Agartala is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, showcasing beautiful architecture and spiritual significance.

Matabari

Home to the famous Tripura Sundari Temple, Matabari is an important pilgrimage site where devotees gather to worship the goddess.

Neer Mahal

Built on the Rudrasagar Lake, Neer Mahal is a stunning water palace combining Hindu and Islamic architectural styles.

Heritage Park

A well-maintained park in Agartala that showcases Tripura's culture, flora, and heritage through various displays and installations.

Unakoti

Known for its ancient rock carvings and sculptures, Unakoti is a sacred pilgrimage site with intriguing stone art and legends.

Buddhist Site in Pilak

Pilak is an archaeological site with ancient Buddhist sculptures, carvings, and ruins, offering insights into Tripura's history.

Jampui Hills

Famous for its scenic beauty, orange orchards, and panoramic views, Jampui Hills is an ideal destination for nature lovers and trekkers.
