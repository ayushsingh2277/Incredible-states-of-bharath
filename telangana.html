Tourist Places in Telangana

Kanakai Waterfalls

Known for its scenic beauty, Kanakai Waterfalls is a peaceful spot ideal for nature lovers and those seeking a refreshing retreat.

Hyderabad

The capital of Telangana, Hyderabad is famous for its historic landmarks, including the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and its vibrant bazaars.

Papikondalu

A picturesque mountain range along the Godavari River, Papikondalu offers scenic boat rides and stunning natural landscapes.

Nagarjunasagar

Home to one of the largest dams in India, Nagarjunasagar is known for its impressive reservoir and Buddhist archaeological sites.

Warangal

Once the capital of the Kakatiya dynasty, Warangal is famed for its ancient temples, including the grand Warangal Fort and Thousand Pillar Temple.

Bhadrachalam

A significant Hindu pilgrimage site, Bhadrachalam is known for its Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple dedicated to Lord Rama.

Nalgonda

A historic city, Nalgonda boasts ancient forts and temples, with scenic landscapes and hillocks surrounding it.

Nizamabad

Known for its forts, temples, and the serene Alisagar Reservoir, Nizamabad combines history with natural beauty.

Secunderabad

Known as Hyderabad's twin city, Secunderabad has a distinct British colonial influence and is known for its Cantonment area and Hussain Sagar Lake.
