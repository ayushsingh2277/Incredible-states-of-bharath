Hoshiarpur

Hoshiarpur is a serene city in Punjab, India, known for its picturesque landscapes, religious significance, and cultural richness. Nestled in the Shivalik foothills, it offers visitors a blend of nature, history, and spirituality, making it a peaceful yet engaging destination.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Takhni-Rehmapur Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Sheesh Mahal (Mubarak Mosque)
  • Damsal Dam
  • Gurdwara Garna Sahib
  • Kamahi Devi Temple
