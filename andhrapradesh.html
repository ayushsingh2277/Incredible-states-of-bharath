Tourist Places in Andhra Pradesh

Vishakapatnam

A beautiful coastal city known for its serene beaches, hilltop parks, and rich cultural heritage.

Araku Valley

A picturesque hill station with breathtaking landscapes, coffee plantations, and tribal culture.

Tirupati

Home to the world-renowned Venkateswara Temple, a major pilgrimage site in India.

Vijayawada

An important city with historic temples, the Krishna River, and a vibrant culture.

Chittoor

Known for its agriculture, temples, and scenic beauty, including the Horsley Hills.

Srisailam

A spiritual destination with the famous Srisailam temple and surrounding natural beauty.

Ongole

A city known for its cattle breed, temples, and proximity to the Bay of Bengal.

Tadipatri

Famous for its ancient temples, with intricate carvings and rich history.

Puttaparthi

A spiritual hub known for the ashram of Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

Yanam

A charming town with French colonial influence, situated on the banks of the Godavari River.

Guntur

A major city known for its historic significance, temples, and agriculture.
