Tourist Places in Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake

A high-altitude glacial lake, Tsomgo is famous for its breathtaking views and is revered by locals. During winter, the lake freezes, offering a surreal experience.

Yumthang Valley

Known as the 'Valley of Flowers,' Yumthang Valley is home to vibrant rhododendrons and stunning alpine scenery. Best visited in spring for full blooms.

Ravangla

Offering panoramic views of the Himalayas, Ravangla is a tranquil hill station. It’s also known for Buddha Park, which houses a towering Buddha statue.

Khangchendzonga National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage site, the park is rich in biodiversity and offers views of the majestic Kanchenjunga peak. Ideal for trekking and wildlife spotting.

Lachung

A charming mountain village, Lachung serves as a gateway to the Yumthang Valley. Known for its apple orchards and serene beauty.

Yuksom

Once the capital of Sikkim, Yuksom is rich in history and is the starting point for treks to Mount Kanchenjunga. Known for its monasteries and natural beauty.

Nathula Pass

At 4,310 meters above sea level, this historic pass connects India and Tibet. Known for its scenic landscape and strategic importance.

Gangtok

Sikkim’s vibrant capital, Gangtok offers bustling markets, monasteries, and stunning views of Mount Kanchenjunga. A perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Pelling

A popular tourist town, Pelling offers clear views of Mount Kanchenjunga and is home to historic sites like the Pemayangtse Monastery.
