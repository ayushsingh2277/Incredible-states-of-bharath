Chandigarh

Chandigarh, known as “The City Beautiful,” is a well-planned and modern city in India, designed by the Swiss-French architect Le Corbusier. It serves as the capital of both Punjab and Haryana and is known for its organized layout, abundant greenery, and unique architectural heritage. Chandigarh offers a blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty, making it a popular destination for tourists.

Top tourist attractions: Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Zakir Hussain Rose Garden, International Dolls Museum, Pinjore Garden

Places to stay: Hotel Pride, Hotel Empire Inn, Hotel Oyester, Ramada Plaza by Wyndham Chandigarh Zirakpur
