Tourist Places in Mizoram

Aizawl

Aizawl is the scenic capital city of Mizoram, offering panoramic views of lush hills and vibrant local culture.

Lunglei

Known for its picturesque landscapes, Lunglei is ideal for nature lovers and trekkers seeking serene beauty.

Mamit

Mamit is popular for its dense forests, wildlife sanctuaries, and cultural villages, offering rich biodiversity.

Kolasib

Known for its scenic charm and lush greenery, Kolasib offers a peaceful escape and agricultural vistas.

Reiek

Reiek is a popular trekking spot, known for its panoramic views and cultural festivals in Mizoram.

Vantawng Waterfalls

Vantawng is Mizoram's highest waterfall, surrounded by thick bamboo forests, perfect for adventure lovers.

Falkawn Village

Falkawn is a traditional Mizo village that offers an insight into the rich heritage and customs of the Mizo people.

Phawngpui

Phawngpui, also known as Blue Mountain, is the highest peak in Mizoram, renowned for its scenic beauty and orchids.
