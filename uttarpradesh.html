Tourist Places in Uttar Pradesh

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra is a city steeped in history and architectural marvels.

Varanasi

One of the oldest inhabited cities, Varanasi is known for its ghats and spiritual significance.

Ayodhya

A historic city revered as the birthplace of Lord Rama, rich in religious and cultural heritage.

Mathura

Famed as the birthplace of Lord Krishna, Mathura is a hub for devotees and tourists alike.

Vrindavan

Known for its temples and as the childhood home of Lord Krishna, Vrindavan attracts many visitors.

Prayagraj

Famous for the Kumbh Mela, Prayagraj is where the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati meet.

Sarnath

A significant Buddhist site where Gautama Buddha delivered his first sermon after enlightenment.

Lucknow

Renowned for its unique culture, architecture, and delicious cuisine, the city offers a royal experience.

Jhansi

A city steeped in history, known for its fort and the valiant Rani Lakshmibai.

Kushinagar

A key Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha attained Mahaparinirvana.

Bulandshahr

A vibrant city with a rich history, often regarded as the gateway to the Doab region.

Ramnagar Fort

This fort is known for its impressive architecture and is situated near Varanasi.
