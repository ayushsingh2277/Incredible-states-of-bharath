Tourist Places in Arunachal Pradesh

Bhalukpong

A gateway to Arunachal Pradesh, Bhalukpong is famous for its picturesque landscapes and adventure activities like rafting and trekking.

Bomdila

A scenic hill station offering panoramic views of the Himalayas and a rich Buddhist culture.

Dirang

A serene valley known for its pleasant weather, natural beauty, and ancient monasteries.

Gorichen Peak

One of the highest peaks in Arunachal Pradesh, offering challenging trekking opportunities.

Itanagar

The capital city of Arunachal Pradesh, known for its historic Ita Fort and Ganga Lake.

Namdapha National Park

A biodiversity hotspot and a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Nuranang Falls

One of the most stunning waterfalls in Arunachal Pradesh, located near Tawang.

Pakhui Wildlife Sanctuary

A sanctuary known for its tigers and diverse flora and fauna.

Pasighat

The oldest town in Arunachal Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty and adventure sports.

Roing

A picturesque town known for its lakes, valleys, and historical sites.

Sangti Valley

A tranquil valley famous for its natural beauty and migratory black-necked cranes.

Sela Pass

A high-altitude mountain pass offering stunning views of snow-capped peaks.
