Tourist Places in Odisha

Puri

Puri is a coastal city known for its ancient Jagannath Temple and scenic beach, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike.

Bhubaneswar

Known as the "Temple City of India," Bhubaneswar is famous for its historic temples and rich cultural heritage.

Konark

Konark is celebrated for its Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an architectural marvel.

Chilika Lake

The largest coastal lagoon in India, Chilika Lake is a haven for migratory birds and a paradise for nature lovers.

Udayagiri

Famous for its rock-cut caves, Udayagiri showcases ancient inscriptions and Buddhist architecture.

Cuttack

Known as the Silver City, Cuttack is renowned for its silver filigree craftsmanship and historic forts.

Bhitarkanika National Park

This lush mangrove forest and wildlife sanctuary is famous for crocodiles, diverse fauna, and scenic views.

Daringbadi

Known as the "Kashmir of Odisha," Daringbadi is a hill station with pine forests and stunning landscapes.

Talasari Beach

A tranquil beach with silvery sands and a scenic view, Talasari Beach is perfect for a peaceful retreat.
